Learning disabilities are neurological differences in processing information that severely limit a person's ability to learn in a specific skill area.
Attention deficit hyperactivity disorder (ADHD) is not a learning disability, but it definitely can affect a person's ability to learn.
Your child is not defined by his or her learning disability.
Parents and preschool teachers are often the first to see early signs of learning disabilities.
Parents of children with learning disabilities need to recognize that children with learning disabilities require exceptional amounts of energy.
