"Cyber bullying is defined as online material that threatens or raises concerns about violence against others, suicide or other selfharm.
Cyber bullying is defined as the use of online tools to harass someone.
Cyber-bullying (v.) :: when a child, preteen or teen is tormented, threatened, harassed, humiliated, embarrassed or otherwise targeted by another child, preteen or teen using the Internet, interactive and digital technologies or mobile phones.
Cyber bullying is a very difficult form of bullying to prevent and to police.
