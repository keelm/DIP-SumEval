Parents have to make sure their kids are committed to their online home school programs, which can be a daunting task considering that they do not have fixed standards of discipline to follow.
Parents starting students with home schooling in ninth grade may discover their students need more self-discipline and good study habits than they anticipated.
And when students are unruly or even violent, who can blame teachers and administrators for punishing them, using the methods that are spelled out in the school's code of conduct.
